 This game will have the formula of a catch game with a the sliding sprite at the bottom of the
 screen but it will be rhythm focused, the things falling will have different colours and create
 different sounds. you will be playing along with a song, having to catch the correct notes at 
 certain stages of the song, which will be indicated to the player
...
30 second  gameplay description
...

 The game starts off with a bgm going off as the player assumes control of an instrument, the 
 player will then try catch notes falling down that are a similar colour to the sprite the player
 is using, Catching the notes will make a beat and the goal is to catch notes to play a full song
 and get a high score
...

Pillars
... The main pillars of this game will be music, difficulty and smooth sprite animations. music will be partly original and also licenced music. 
The sprites will be instruement that play music when they catch the notes, need to be satifying for the player. 
Difficulty will be increased over time or through levels.
